Tips to get organised


1. Do not put off organizing for later.

  • Do it now, at this moment.
  • The longer you wait to do something, the more difficult it will be to get it done.


2. Write things down.

  • Don't expect that you will remember everything.


3. Stay organized.

  • Make a to-do list.
  • Cross out items as you complete them, so you feel productive and motivated to tackle more items on the list.


4. Find time to organise space around you.

  • It is important to declutter your life.


5. Don't spend too much time on completing your Bullet Journal or different planner.

  • Try to make it simple but useful.
